The Unrelease! a.k.a. changelog

My release schedule is not what you typically see. Rather than waiting and bundling a bunch of cool stuff for a major version. I release them as they are ready and the branding and celebration comes towards the end of the year, ready to explore new adventure in the year to come.

Explore the release celebration as part of my year in review each year!

This site is designed in a sketchbook, Sketch & Figma, built using Jekyll, Foundation CSS and Github Pages for hosting. Adobe Photoshop and Affinity Photo were fired up for images and photography and later optimzied in ImageOptim.

Interested in how the code changes year to year?

Head over to the Github repository.
